# Cold Wake Training Fixture Notes

Cold Wake is useful to Ghostlight as a grounded historical pressure cooker, not
as the active product target.

The deleted `aetheria-cold-wake-mvp-scenario.md` brief had the right social
machine in it: Ganymede corridor law, thermal stealth panic, Navigator rescue
obligation, insurer category failure, shadow cognition, and institutions forced
to translate the same ambiguous vessel through incompatible moral economies.
The bad part was the label. "MVP scenario" quietly points the repo back toward
building a game slice when the current target is projection, content generation,
and dialogue scaffolding.

So the revived shape is narrower:

- use Cold Wake as an authored historical Aetheria flashpoint
- distill it into lore grounding digests
- generate projection fixtures from specific rooms inside the crisis
- keep speaker prompts local to what each participant knows, believes, fears,
  misreads, and needs
- do not let this become the current Ghostlight game plan by accident

## Why Cold Wake Works

Cold Wake has the right kind of ambiguity for Ghostlight.

A quiet-running vessel enters the Jovian route crisis under classifications that
do not line up. Thermal telemetry is contradictory. Insurers want categories to
mean something again. Navigators hear a rescue obligation vibrating under the
paperwork. Lightsail needs the convoy moving. Aya-coded care workers suspect a
person is being pushed out of the ledger. Pirates smell both opportunity and
closure. Cognitum cutouts want every possible mind reduced back into product
language. Cryonix and Rossum actors know the corridor now depends on technical
margins most people only understand after they fail.

That lets Ghostlight test exactly the thing it exists to model: dialogue and
drama emerging from state, pressure, memory, culture, incentives, and partial
perception rather than one big narrator voice wearing character masks.

## Candidate First Rooms

- PSC risk tribunal freezes a quiet-running vessel's corridor status while a
  Navigator route steward argues rescue cannot wait for category repair.
- Lightsail dispatch needs to move a convoy before heat-debt windows and insurer
  rulings collide.
- Aya-aligned sanctuary intake receives a distress claim that may be refugee,
  pirate deception, Cognitum contraband, or all three with worse paperwork.
- A Cognitum shell vendor insists an implicated routing artifact is only a
  derivative product while its behavior keeps looking distressingly like
  testimony.
- A Cryonix or Rossum certification rep refuses to bless an improvised maneuver
  that might save lives but would rupture the declared envelope.

## Boundary

Cold Wake can be a training fixture because it is pre-Elysium historical lore.
That makes it better for grounded projection training than malleable Elysium
branch material.

Cold Wake should not overwrite the current Aetheria consumer target: Call of the
Void content generation and narrated timeline procedural drama. This is feedstock
for Ghostlight's machinery, not the machine's destination.
